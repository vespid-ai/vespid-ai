# Launch notes for the first vespid.ai shell

The first version optimizes for three things:

1. a homepage that can carry a stronger brand voice
2. a docs system that will not collapse as content grows
3. a workflow simple enough to keep writing in

## Why not build from scratch?

Because the hard part is not rendering HTML. The hard part is maintaining a coherent writing and navigation system as projects multiply.

## Why this stack?

Astro gives us freedom for the surface.
Starlight gives us discipline for the reading experience.

That combination is the right default until the site proves it has needs the existing system cannot meet.
