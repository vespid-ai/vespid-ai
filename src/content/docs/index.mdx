<div class="vespid-section">
  <div class="vespid-meta">Why GEO matters</div>
  <h2>Public visibility now depends on machine-readable structure</h2>
  <p>
    A strong product can still disappear in AI search if its public surface is ambiguous, fragmented,
    or impossible for retrieval systems to extract cleanly. GEO is the discipline of fixing that with
    better artifacts, clearer pages, and explicit machine-readable signals.
  </p>
